EXIF Frame

Drop in a photo and download it with a clean frame showing the camera and shooting settings — focal length, aperture, shutter, ISO. Everything runs in your browser; nothing is uploaded.

Click to choose photos, or drag & drop them here JPEG keeps the most EXIF data. Other formats still get framed (without settings).

No photos yet — add some above.

Tip: the frame scales with each photo, so the text stays proportional whether the shot is landscape or portrait. Output is a lossless PNG at the photo’s full resolution — great quality, but the files can be large.
